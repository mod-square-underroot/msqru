About me

A wanderer between thought and fracture. I write to make sense of distortions, to shape rhythm out of dissonance. Here lies a fragment of every dream that tried to make itself real — and every reality that wished to be a dream.

The works within this space oscillate between clarity and chaos. They’re not meant to be read — they’re meant to be *felt*.
